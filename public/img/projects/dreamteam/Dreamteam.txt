Dreamteam

Dreamteam is a theater group from Vienna. I had the opportunity to take care of their branding for their first performance, starting with the logo. After the logo was done, I created a complete package including flyers, posters, a program booklet, gift cards, and more for their debut performance titled "Der Muss Es Sein."
